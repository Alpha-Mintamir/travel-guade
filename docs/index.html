Introduction

Welcome to Travel Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Travel Buddy, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use Travel Buddy, we collect information that you voluntarily provide:

1. Account Information
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • City of residence (optional)
  • Bio/description (optional)
  • Travel preferences (optional)
2. Trip Information
  • Destination names
  • Travel dates
  • Trip descriptions
  • Trip photos (destination and personal photos)
  • Budget preferences
  • Travel style preferences
  • Contact information (Instagram username, phone number, Telegram username)
3. Communication Data
  • Messages sent through the in-app chat feature
  • Trip join requests

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data
  • Log data and error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide and Maintain the Service

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your trips to other users
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Process trip join requests

2. Improve Our Service

  • Understand how users interact with our App
  • Fix bugs and improve functionality
  • Develop new features

3. Communicate With You

  • Send notifications about trip requests and messages
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important service updates

4. Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the safety of our users

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Information Visible to Other Users

When you use Travel Buddy, certain information is visible to other users:

  • Your profile name and photo
  • Trips you create (including destination, dates, description, and photos)
  • Contact information you choose to share on trips (Instagram, phone, Telegram)

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our App:

  • Cloud Storage: Cloudinary (for image storage)
  • Database: Neon PostgreSQL (for data storage)
  • Email Services: Resend (for email notifications)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication using industry-standard practices
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  1. Access: View your personal information in the App
  2. Update: Edit your profile and trip information
  3. Delete: Delete your trips or request account deletion
  4. Opt-out: Disable notifications in your device settings

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us using the information below.

Travel Buddy is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country of residence. By using our App, you consent to this transfer.
