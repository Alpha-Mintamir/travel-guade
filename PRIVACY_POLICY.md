# Privacy Policy for Travel Buddy

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Travel Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Travel Buddy, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Travel Buddy, we collect information that you voluntarily provide:

1. **Account Information**
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Profile photo (optional)
   - City of residence (optional)
   - Bio/description (optional)
   - Travel preferences (optional)

2. **Trip Information**
   - Destination names
   - Travel dates
   - Trip descriptions
   - Trip photos (destination and personal photos)
   - Budget preferences
   - Travel style preferences
   - Contact information (Instagram username, phone number, Telegram username)

3. **Communication Data**
   - Messages sent through the in-app chat feature
   - Trip join requests

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect:

- Device information (device type, operating system)
- App usage data
- Log data and error reports

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the Service**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Display your trips to other users
   - Facilitate communication between users
   - Process trip join requests

2. **Improve Our Service**
   - Understand how users interact with our App
   - Fix bugs and improve functionality
   - Develop new features

3. **Communicate With You**
   - Send notifications about trip requests and messages
   - Respond to your inquiries and support requests
   - Send important service updates

4. **Safety and Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Protect the safety of our users
